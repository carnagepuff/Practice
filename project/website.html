Cybersecurity is an important area, as with the increase in the number of cyber threats, it becomes important to protect information from cyber attacks.

3 main aspects of CS :

1
Technical aspect.

The technical aspect of cybersecurity involves implementing technologies and measures such as antivirus software, firewalls, intrusion detection systems, and encryption to protect information systems and data from cyber threats.

2
Organizational aspect.

The organizational aspect of cybersecurity focuses on establishing rules, policies, and procedures to ensure the security of information systems and data, including data classification and protection, access management and incident response planning.

3
Human aspect.

The human aspect of cybersecurity addresses the behavior and actions of individuals in the context of using information technologies, emphasizing user education and awareness, and mitigating risks associated with human errors and social engineering attacks.

Main Features & Benefits


Confidentiality.

Cybersecurity ensures the confidentiality of sensitive information by preventing unauthorized access or disclosure, protecting it from cybercriminals, competitors, or other malicious actors.

Integrity.

Cybersecurity measures safeguard the integrity of data and information systems. It ensures that data remains unaltered and accurate throughout its lifecycle, protecting against unauthorized modifications.

Availability.

Cybersecurity measures aim to ensure the availability of critical systems and services. By protecting against cyber threats, such as denial-of-service (DoS) attacks or system failures, cybersecurity helps maintain the continuous operation of networks.

Resilience.

Cybersecurity promotes resilience by establishing mechanisms to detect, respond to, and recover from cyber incidents. It includes proactive measures like incident response planning, data backup, and disaster recovery strategies.
